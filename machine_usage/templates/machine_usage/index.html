THE FORGE


The Forge provides a collaborative environment for RPI's design community. We promote communication and critical thinking, as well as STEAM (Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts, Mathematics) ideals. We provide easy everyday access to tools that help with rapid prototyping and communication/collaboration, as well as a casual area to work. We also hold workshops, design competitions, and outreach events; and host speakers involved in the maker movement.


We are Rensselaer Polytechnic Institutes only interdisciplinary makerspace, and are 100% student run!
